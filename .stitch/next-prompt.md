A high-impact, premium landing page hero for RoadMate.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Vibe**: Luxury, minimal, high-contrast, premium automotive feel.
- **Palette**: Pure White (#FFFFFF), Deep Obsidian (#0F172A), Crimson Red (#E11D48).
- **Styles**: Softly rounded (16px), Glassmorphism (blur 15px, 0.1 opacity borders), whisper-soft shadows.
- **Typography**: Inter, 900 weight headings, tight tracking.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header**: Sticky glassmorphism Navbar with logo left and navigation/Account button right.
2. **Hero Hero Content**: 
   - Massive headline: "Rent Premium Bikes & Cars" (with Crimson accent).
   - Subtext: "Experience the road with elite performance and unparalleled style."
3. **Search Card**: A floating glassmorphism card with fields for Location, Dates, and Times.
4. **Action**: "Search Availability" button overlapping the bottom edge of the card in Crimson Red.
5. **Stats Ribbon**: A sleek dark ribbon below the hero showing "500+ Vehicles", "24/7 Support", "Secure Payments".

**Visual Notes:**
- Use high-fidelity photography of a luxury car (e.g., Porsche or Lamborghini) in the background.
- Subtle micro-animations on hover for all interactive elements.
